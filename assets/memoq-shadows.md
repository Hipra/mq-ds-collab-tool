## Pattern

All shadows follow this pattern:

- **Color:** `rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.08)`
- **Offset X:** `0px`
- **Offset Y:** `{n}px` (equals the elevation level)
- **Blur:** `{n*2}px` (double the elevation level)
- **Spread:** `0px`
